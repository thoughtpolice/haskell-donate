Donate with Stripe

To donate to Haskell.org immediately, the preferred method is to use Stripe with our online form. This is easy, convenient, and should work in any modern browser (on mobile, or desktop).

Stripe supports credit and debit cards, as well as Bitcoin. We even support in-browser Apple Pay.

To donate, click here and use the quick checkout form. You can give us money unbelievably quickly!
